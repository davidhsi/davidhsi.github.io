Chicago Bulls Visualization Project

  • All visualizations where created in RStudio to show the Bulls defensive statistics, new player scoring, and overall ability of the Chicago Bulls
  • Created histograms to visualize and examine the progression of Bull's steals, blocks, and defensive rebounds from 1999-2021
  • Produced 4 linegraphs to analyze the improvement in scoring ability of Demar DeRozan, Alex Caruso, Lonzo Ball, and Nikola Vucevic throughout their careers
  • Created radar plots measuring assists, true shooting percentage, usage percentage, box plus-minus, and total rebound percentage of Zach Lavine in the 2021-2022 season and Cody White in the 2020-2021 season
